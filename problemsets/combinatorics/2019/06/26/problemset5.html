    Week 5 Problem Sets

    Did you know that the popular 3x3x3 Rubik’s cube has 43,252,003,274,489,856,000 valid permutations? It means that it’s nearly impossible to predict the state of a scrambled cube. Yet people have managed to solve it in (way) less than 5 seconds.

    Combinatorics problems in competitive programming is always fun to solve, albeit sometimes difficult. If you can find the correct formula/pattern, your solution can be very short. They could also be good practice for Math 240, too!
